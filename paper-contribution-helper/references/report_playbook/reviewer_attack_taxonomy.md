# Reviewer Attack Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy for Reviewer Attack Preplay and manuscript trigger localization.

## High-Impact Concerns

- novelty / incrementality;
- A+B/C combination or arbitrary assembly;
- baseline fairness and comparison contract;
- mechanism evidence and whether ablation proves necessity;
- experiment scope, robustness, and stress coverage;
- cost, efficiency, and deployment tradeoff;
- reproducibility, implementation detail, and protocol clarity;
- claim exceeding evidence;
- related work boundary and closest-prior-work handling;
- AC/meta-review style concern: whether the paper has a coherent contribution story.

## Required Analysis For Each Attack

For each concern, include:

- why a reviewer will ask it;
- exact manuscript trigger;
- strong defense posture;
- no-new-experiment repair;
- Tier 1 reuse option if logs, appendix, or existing figures can help;
- Tier 2 evidence if the claim cannot be defended otherwise.

Do not list concerns without repairs.
