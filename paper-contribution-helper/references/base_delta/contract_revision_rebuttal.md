# Contract: Revision And Rebuttal

For each reviewer concern:

| Concern | Why reviewer asks | Trigger | Defense posture | Tier 0 | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Residual risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Always include:

- `Tier 0: 不增加实验`
- `Tier 1: 复用已有材料`
- `Tier 2: 新增强证据`

English rebuttal snippets must be strong but evidence-bounded.
