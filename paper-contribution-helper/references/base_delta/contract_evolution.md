# Contract: Skill Evolution

New examples are classified as:

- `include`
- `manual-review`
- `exclude`

Include only samples that teach reusable methodology for low-novelty, incremental, A+B/C, light-transfer, method-migration, engineering-optimization, or evidence-system papers.

Every candidate example must be analyzed through `evolve_from_paper.py` or an equivalent standard material-folder workflow before it can affect the skill. The evolution record must include PDF analysis status, review/reply status, claim-delta matrix, reviewer-attack analysis, and a saved per-paper report.

If reviews are missing, simulate likely attacks and label them `simulated-review`.

Append only anonymized case cards to evolving casebook.

Do not append a casebook card directly from a title, abstract, or memory. The saved per-paper intermediate report is the minimum evidence unit.
